The Mission of the Church

The Church of Uganda is committed to spreading the gospel, promoting community development, and nurturing spiritual growth.

We empower local leaders to carry out Christ-centered missions, provide access to education and clean water, and organize outreach programs to uplift the most vulnerable in society. Our vision is to see transformed lives through spiritual and social care rooted in faith, love, and action.

Serving the Community

Through education, healthcare, and faith, the Church of Uganda continues to uplift communities and spread hope.

By volunteering or supporting our projects, you help create sustainable impact — building schools, training health workers, and supporting families in need.

The Church of Uganda, founded on the Gospel of Jesus Christ and guided by Anglican tradition, is committed to proclaiming the Good News, building vibrant communities, and transforming lives through worship, discipleship, education, and service to all people.

Our Vision

A Christ-centered, Spirit-led, and Bible-based Church that is united, self-sustaining, and deeply rooted in faith and social responsibility; a beacon of hope transforming individuals, families, and communities across Uganda and beyond for the glory of God.

Our Mission

To fulfill the Great Commission by preaching the Gospel of salvation through Jesus Christ, nurturing believers in spiritual maturity, promoting servant leadership, and addressing the holistic needs of society—including health, education, peace, and justice—while upholding the values of love, integrity, and accountability.

Our Aim

To be a transformative Church that empowers the faithful through sound biblical teaching, inclusive worship, and life-changing ministries. We aim to develop spiritually mature Christians who are equipped for service, leadership, stewardship, and active participation in both Church and nation-building.

In all things, the Church of Uganda seeks to glorify God through faithful worship, compassionate outreach, unity in diversity, and a steadfast commitment to the spiritual and physical well-being of all God’s people.
